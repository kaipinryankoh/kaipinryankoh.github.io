About Me

I'm a third-year Computer Science undergraduate at UC Berkeley with an interest in Artificial Intelligence and software development. I'm also currently an Undergraduate Student Instructor for Berkeley's Intro to Artificial Intelligence class CS188.

I've been involved in leadership for several clubs on campus, including Pioneers in Engineering and Computer Science Mentors. Additionally, I've been part of the computer science honor society UPE since my sophomore year.

In my spare time, I enjoy dancing with AFX and playing video games with friends!
